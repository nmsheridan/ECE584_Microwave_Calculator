The ECE584 Microwave Calculator

Written in 2018 for the Microwave Engineering course at the University of Massachusetts Amherst.
The formulas in the software originate for Microwave Engineering by Professor David M. Pozar and from the lectures and notes of Professor Marinos N. Vouvakis.

Written by N. Michael Sheridan of the University of Massachusetts Amherst and BAE Systems Information and Electronic Systems Inc.
